About us

Welcome to ExploreMTB, the premier mountain bike rental company dedicated to helping you explore the great outdoors on two wheels. We are a team of passionate riders and guides who are committed to providing excellent customer service and creating unforgettable experiences for our customers.

At ExploreMTB, we understand that every rider is unique, which is why we offer a wide range of top-of-the-line mountain bikes to suit all skill levels and fitness levels. Whether you're a seasoned pro or a first-time rider, our team of experienced guides will work with you to ensure that you have the best experience possible. They will help you choose the right bike for you, provide you with all the information and gear you need, and guide you on the best trails for your abilities.

We are committed to sustainability and environmental responsibility. We believe that it is important to preserve and protect the natural beauty of the mountains, and we are dedicated to reducing our carbon footprint and minimizing our impact on the environment

We have something for everyone, whether you're looking for a fun day out with friends, a romantic getaway with your significant other, or an adventure-filled family vacation. Our motto "Sick Rides & Good Vibes" is evident in everything we do, and we guarantee that you will have an unforgettable experience with us.

So why wait? Book your mountain bike rental today and get ready to explore the mountains like never before! We look forward to riding with you.
